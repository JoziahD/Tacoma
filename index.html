Trim Levels

The 3rd Gen Tacoma was offered in six main trim levels, each targeting a different buyer.

SR

Base

The entry-level SR comes with the essentials ---- a reliable workhorse with a 2.7L 4-cylinder or available 3.5L V6 engine.

  • Steel wheels (16")
  • 6-speed automatic or manual transmission
  • Vinyl flooring
  • Toyota Safety Sense-P (2018+)
  • Available in Access Cab or Double Cab

Starting MSRP: ~$26,400

SR5

Popular Value

The SR5 adds comfort and convenience features over the base SR, making it the most popular choice for everyday drivers.

  • 17" alloy wheels
  • Upgraded infotainment with 7" touchscreen
  • Rear-view camera
  • Carpet flooring
  • Sport appearance package available

Starting MSRP: ~$31,000

TRD Sport

Street Performance

Focused on road performance and sporty styling. The TRD Sport delivers an aggressive look with upgraded suspension.

  • 3.5L V6 engine (standard)
  • Sport-tuned suspension
  • Hood scoop
  • Fox shocks (2020+, select packages)
  • Available in exclusive Sport colors

Starting MSRP: ~$35,000

TRD Off-Road

Trail Ready

Built for the trails. The TRD Off-Road is one of the most sought-after trims for off-road enthusiasts thanks to its factory-ready 4x4 capability.

  • Crawl Control
  • Multi-Terrain Select
  • Locking rear differential
  • Bilstein shocks
  • All-terrain tires on 16" wheels

Starting MSRP: ~$37,000

Limited

Luxury

The most refined Tacoma on the lot. The Limited is loaded with premium features for a truck that doubles as a luxury daily driver.

  • Leather-trimmed seats (heated front)
  • 8" touchscreen with navigation
  • JBL premium audio
  • Power-adjustable driver seat
  • Premium 18" alloy wheels

Starting MSRP: ~$42,000

TRD Pro

Flagship Off-Road

The crown jewel of the Tacoma lineup. The TRD Pro is purpose-built for extreme off-road performance with iconic all-terrain style.

  • Fox 2.5" internal bypass shocks
  • Old Man Emu (OME) front springs
  • Skid plates (full set)
  • TRD Cat-back exhaust
  • Exclusive color options (e.g., Army Green, Lunar Rock)

Starting MSRP: ~$47,000
